Larry looked at Don. They had been best friends since their first year at Pogtarts.
Penelope, their new classmate, shook her head at the two boys. She considered them worse students than her.
Slate was one of their professors, hated by our boy Donnie. He was appointed by the previous director.
Jumblelore was the current director of the school, admired by Penelope. Slate was always trying to get his job.

But the second year, Larry and Don had a fight and became enemies. Slate successfully got Jumblelore fired and took his position as the school director.
Slate appointed his friend Lucas for his former teaching position. And Penelope became friend of Larry and Don.
During class, Larry, Don and Penelope felt Lucas was not a good teacher.

The third year, Larry and Don became friends again, so Penelope felt relieved that her friends reconciled.
Jumblelore got his position as Director of Pogtarts back from Slate, who was fired as a teacher along with Lucas.
In place of Slate, Jumblelore appointed Harold as teacher of Don, Larry and Penelope.
